A Deeper Understanding of the SX — 8

You are passionate, intense and charismatic, with great personal magnetism. When you go after a goal or ideal you tend to have a big impact on the environment.

You may act impulsively and provocatively, standing out as a rebel or trail blazer. Material things do not particularly interest you as much as power, attention, and influence.

You collect a small group of trusted people around you and are a loyal, reliable and protective friend.
